Your Safety, Our Priority

About Us

Absolute Security Limited is a proudly Zambian-owned company committed to providing reliable, innovative, and cost-effective security solutions. With a highly trained team and a client-focused approach, we specialize in safeguarding homes, businesses, and digital assets. Our strength lies in blending human expertise with modern security technologies such as CCTV systems, access control, alarm systems, and cybersecurity tools. We serve a wide range of sectors — from residential estates and corporate facilities to government institutions. At the heart of our operations is a passion for protecting people and property, ensuring that safety is not just a promise, but a guarantee.

Our Services

Residential Security

Our Residential Security services provide comprehensive protection through a combination of trained security personnel and advanced technology. We deploy 24/7 manned guarding, mobile patrols, and rapid response teams to ensure immediate support in case of any incident. Our systems include CCTV surveillance, motion detectors, alarm systems, electric fencing, and remote monitoring to deter intrusions and keep your family safe. Whether you reside in a private residence, apartment, or gated community, we tailor our solutions to suit your lifestyle and security needs, ensuring peace of mind at all times.

Commercial Security

Absolute Security Limited offers robust Commercial Security solutions tailored to protect your business, assets, and personnel. Whether you operate a retail outlet, corporate office, warehouse, financial institution, or industrial site, we deliver reliable protection through highly trained guards, perimeter control, 24/7 CCTV surveillance, and electronic access systems. Our services also include alarm monitoring, armed response, visitor screening, and scheduled patrols. By leveraging modern security technology — such as motion sensors, biometric access control, and remote video surveillance — we reduce vulnerabilities and ensure quick response to potential threats. With a focus on professionalism, discretion, and compliance with Zambian security regulations, we help you maintain a secure and productive business environment.

Cybersecurity

In today’s digital era, the threats to your business are not just physical — they’re virtual too. Absolute Security Limited provides cutting-edge Cybersecurity solutions designed to safeguard your company’s sensitive data, IT infrastructure, and digital presence. Our services protect against data breaches, malware, phishing attacks, ransomware, and unauthorized access to your systems and websites. Whether you’re running a corporate website, an e-commerce store, or internal portals, we ensure they are hardened against cyber threats. We help businesses implement SSL certificates, secure hosting configurations, and web application firewalls. Our team also conducts vulnerability assessments to identify weak points and keep your online platforms compliant and protected.
